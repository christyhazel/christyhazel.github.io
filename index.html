About Me:

From 2020-2023 I was a Hedrick Assistant Adjunct Professor at UCLA in the mathematics department working with Mike Hill. I'm also involved with the electronic Computational Homotopy Theory (eCHT) online research community and am currently supported as a National Science Foundation (NSF) eCHT Reasearch Training Group Postdoctoral Fellow. In Fall of 2023 I will be joining the mathematics department at Grinnell College as an Assistant Professor.

I finished my PhD in 2020 at the University of Oregon under the supervision of Dan Dugger.

My research is in algebraic topology. I'm interested in equivariant homotopy theory, and much of work has focused on RO(G)-graded Bredon cohomology. My "Research" tab contains a summary of my research area, a link to my research statement, and a list of my publications.

In addition to research, I have taught a variety of math classes at UO and UCLA. My "Teaching" tab has information about my classes. I am also currently assisting with an online REU through the eCHT.
